Buttermilk Pancakes

There’s nothing like pancakes to start your day, end your day or enjoy any time in between! My pancake recipe can be served with butter and syrup, and fruit pancakes to quick dinnertime recipes. Pancakes are often considered a weekend-only treat, but there’s no denying that the other five days would be substantially better if they, too, started with a warm short stack. To make this an actually feasible option on hectic mornings, you have a few options. You can make and freeze pancakes in advance (or even freeze the batter), or you could master this one-bowl recipe that requires just six pantry ingredients and is ready in 30 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • 250g flour 🥞
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar🍬
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt🥄
  • 2 cups of milk🥛
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder🤗
  • 2 eggs🥚🥚

Steps

  1. Add flour into a bowl
  2. Mix the flour with salt, sugar and baking powder
  3. Into a separate bowl, beat the eggs
  4. Add the milk to the eggs
  5. Mix the first bowl with the second one
